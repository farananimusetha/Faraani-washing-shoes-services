How We Work

Our step-by-step process ensures every pair of shoes is inspected, treated, cleaned and finished to a high standard.

1. Inspection

Identify material, damage, and stain types to create a care plan.

2. Deep Clean

Use professional solutions, brushes and steam where applicable.

3. Repair & Restore

Repair stitching, re-sole, and perform leather recoloring where needed.

4. Polish & Protect

Final polish and protective coating to extend life.
