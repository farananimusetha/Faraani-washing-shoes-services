About Faranani Shoe Cleaning

We restore footwear, build confidence and provide opportunities for local youth through training and part-time work at our facility in Venda.

Our Mission

Deliver professional shoe care while empowering the next generation through skills training and employment opportunities.

Our Vision

To be Venda’s trusted shoe care provider, known for quality service and social impact.

Our Story

Started locally to meet community needs — now serving customers and reinvesting in local youth programs.

Our Values

Integrity

Honesty in service and pricing.

Quality

Attention to detail and professional standards.

Community

Giving back through training and employment.
